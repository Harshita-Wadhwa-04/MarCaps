Design Your Marketing Organization To Drive Growth

Our Perspective

  1. Current State

    Companies are eager to modernize their marketing departments to adapt to the rapidly changing media landscape, shifting customer preferences, and the rise of new competitors. However, a majority of marketers feel their marketing efforts aren't hitting the mark in driving business impact.

  2. The Problem

    Our research findings highlight three primary factors hindering optimal marketing organization performance:

    1. Absence of a clear shared mission,
    2. Marketing organization capabilities out of sync with growth requirements, and
    3. Operational challenges disrupting smooth workflows.

  3. The Solution

    The MarCaps framework outlines six distinct opportunity areas for modern marketing teams to create value. The framework serves as a foundation and provides an assessment tool, a guide to collaborative choices on mission, capability priorities, and operational improvements to boost marketing driven growth.
